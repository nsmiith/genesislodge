About Genesis Lodge

Genesis Lodge is a supportive home for fully physically functioning males, age 26 and older, who are living with mental health issues and benefit from help with daily living.

Our mission

We start from a simple belief: every person is unique and deserves to feel respected and valued. Our staff and volunteers work alongside the people we support, encouraging them to be the experts in their own lives.

We focus on preserving dignity and self-respect. Support is offered in a way that builds confidence and independence, rather than taking control away from the individual.

How we work

Over the years, Genesis Lodge has built strong relationships with community resources across Brampton. We help residents stay connected to family, friends, healthcare providers, and community programs that support their mental health and recovery.

Our goal is to create a stable, predictable environment where people feel safe, supported, and part of a community—both inside the house and in the wider city.

Who we support

  • Adult men (26+) living with mental health challenges
  • Individuals who are fully physically independent
  • Those who benefit from help with daily routines
  • People who want a calm, structured environment

Our history

The lodge operates in a historic three-storey house on the outskirts of Brampton, originally built in the 19th century and later recognized as a heritage property.

The home was first owned by the Vodden family. In 1986 it was purchased and adapted to provide care for retired individuals. Over time, the need for housing and support for people with mental health issues became more visible in the community.

Genesis Lodge evolved in response to that need. Today, it offers a safe, family-like environment where residents can receive structured support while maintaining as much independence as possible.

Today

  • Heritage three-storey residence
  • Located in Brampton, Ontario
  • Family-style atmosphere
  • Focus on mental health support
