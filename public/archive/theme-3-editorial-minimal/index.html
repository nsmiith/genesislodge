Supportive home in the heart of Brampton

Genesis Lodge is a warm, family-style setting for adult men living with mental health challenges, offering daily support in a heritage home on the edge of the city.

Explore our services Talk to our intake team

Living with dignity, feeling at home

We provide a structured, supportive environment where people can focus on their recovery, maintain daily routines, and stay connected to family, healthcare providers, and community programs.

Our mission

Every person is unique and deserves to feel respected and valued. We support residents in building on their strengths and remaining in control of their own lives.

Heritage home setting

Genesis Lodge operates from a restored 19th-century home recognized as part of Brampton’s heritage, providing a calm and familiar environment.

Coordinated community care

We work closely with physicians, nurses, and community agencies to help residents access the services and supports they need.
