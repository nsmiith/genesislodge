Privacy & Terms of Use

This page explains how Genesis Lodge handles information and the conditions for using this website.

Privacy

Genesis Lodge is committed to respecting the privacy and dignity of residents, families, and visitors. This website is informational only and does not collect sensitive personal health information.

When you contact Genesis Lodge by phone, fax, or email, any information you provide is used only to respond to your inquiry, assess suitability for admission, or coordinate ongoing communication. We do not sell or share this information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Our service providers (such as hosting or email providers) may have access to limited information as needed to operate this website and communication systems. These providers are expected to maintain appropriate safeguards for any information they process on our behalf.

Cookies & Analytics

This website may use basic, privacy-respecting analytics to understand how visitors use the site (for example, which pages are visited most often). These tools typically collect anonymized or aggregated information about page views, device types, and general location.

You can configure your browser to block cookies or notify you when they are being used. Doing so may affect how some websites function, but this site should remain usable with cookies disabled.

Website Use

The content on this website is provided for general information about Genesis Lodge and its services. It is not a substitute for medical, psychiatric, or legal advice. Decisions about care, treatment, or admission should always be made in consultation with qualified professionals.

While we aim to keep information accurate and up to date, details such as availability, services, and policies may change without notice. Genesis Lodge is not responsible for any loss or harm that may arise from reliance on information on this website.

Links to Other Sites

This website may include links to other organizations or resources. These links are provided for convenience only. Genesis Lodge does not control and is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of external websites.
