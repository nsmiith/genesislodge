Who We Are

Genesis Lodge has provided supportive housing for men living with mental health challenges for many years. We offer a safe, structured, and dignified home in a restored heritage property in downtown Brampton.

Our focus is on helping residents maintain stability, build daily living skills, and stay connected to family, community programs, and health-care providers.

Home Like Environment

Shared meals, common spaces, and a family-style atmosphere unlike an institutionalized setting.

Collaborative Supports

We work alongside physicians, clinics, hospitals, and community programs to support residents.

What Residents Can Expect

A structured and stable environment built around safety, dignity, and community.

Support & Health

Medication administration, support with daily routines, and coordination with health-care providers.

  • Registered Nurse On Staff
  • Access to a family physician
  • Pharmacy liaison & wellness monitoring

Daily Living

Residents receive assistance with laundry, housekeeping, and a consistent meal schedule.

  • Nutritious, well-balanced meals
  • Housekeeping services
  • Laundry support

Community & Activities

Individualized activity plans and connections to community outreach and rehabilitative programs.

  • On-site and community activities
  • Support attending programs and appointments
  • Encouragement of family involvement

Life At Genesis Lodge

Each resident has unique strengths, needs, and goals. We work with residents, families, and community partners to create routines that support stability and quality of life.

  • Consistent daily routine and structure
  • Flexible activity program based on interests and abilities
  • Support attending appointments and day programs
  • Opportunities for social connection in a communal environment.
